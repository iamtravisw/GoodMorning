    So what is it?

    Good Morning is a web application that emails you every morning with a motiviational photo. Start your day off with positivity by signing up for our mailing list.

    Read your inspirational quotes during your morning coffee, or wait until you get to work each morning for a little pick me up.

    At any time you can unsubscribe from the mailing list with a link in your daily email. Good Morning does not distribute your personal information to any 3rd parties.
